About

Darshan Safety Zone

DSZ

Darshan Safety Zone , began its operations with the vision to mark its strong presence in the Domestic & international market. Since the 2012 establishment, our company has invested time in research as well as development procedures which further results in quality productions. Excellent customer support, ethical business practices along with an affordable price range are the main factors that have contributed to our business's fast growth.

We are widely acknowledged as an eminent manufacturer and distributor in the market for offering high temperature textiles and sealing products for catering application requirements for thermal insulation, automotive, heat protection, fire protection, high temperature sealing, etc. Apart from this, our range includes Thermal textiles in several fibers like Glass Fiber 600°C, Ceramic Fiber 1260°C, Silicone Coated 550°C, High Silica 1100°C, etc. Further, we also offer Gaskets, Gland Packing and Sheets for valves & pumps in several materials such as PTFE, graphite, rubber and other components.
